Types of Liability Insurance Coverage

At SD Group, we offer a range of liability insurance coverage options to ensure comprehensive protection:

  • Public Liability Insurance: Protects against legal liabilities for injury or damage to third parties.
  • Product Liability Insurance: Covers legal liabilities arising from defective products.
  • Professional Indemnity Insurance: Protects against claims of professional negligence or errors.
  • Employer's Liability Insurance: Covers legal liabilities for employee injuries or illnesses.
